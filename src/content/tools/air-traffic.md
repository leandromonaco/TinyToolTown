Orchestrate GitHub Copilot on your local machine or cloud devbox remotely from your phone via Slack. It runs as a daemon on your dev machines, bridging Slack messages to Copilot's agent SDK. Send prompts, review diffs, manage projects, and stream live agent output, all from a Slack DM while you're away from your desk. You can start a Copilot CLI session locally at your desk, then seamlessly pick it up from your phone, output streams to Slack and you keep prompting remotely.
