Annotation Overlay lets you draw over anything on your screen — live. It sits invisibly on top of all your windows and activates with a click or Cmd+Shift+D. Use it during presentations, pair programming, screen shares, or video calls to circle, underline, and arrow your way through any idea without switching apps. Built because every time you want to point at something on screen, you shouldn't have to open a separate tool, import a screenshot, or interrupt your flow. It just works — pen, arrows, rectangles, highlighter — and gets out of the way when you're done.
