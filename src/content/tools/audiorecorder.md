A Mac audio recorder that gets you recording in seconds.  It captures any input source for long, unattended sessions while providing live level meter, clip detection, sleep prevention, and auto-incrementing filenames so you never overwrite a prior take.  The prep step before Logic Pro, GarageBand, or Audacity, not a replacement for them.
