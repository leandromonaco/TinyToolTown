ClipPing is a lightweight Windows utility that flashes a brief overlay on your active window whenever the clipboard changes. It gives you instant visual confirmation that a copy operation succeeded without interrupting your workflow. The app runs quietly in the system tray, offers six overlay styles, and lets you customize the color and startup behavior.
