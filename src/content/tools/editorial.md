Fighting with imposter syndrome and posting my work, I decided to fight it head on and create my own editorial team of agents. A collaborative multi-agent writing assistant where five specialist AI editors, (Research, Grammar, Fact-Check, Audience, and Style) analyze your content simultaneously, then refine their insights by cross-referencing each other's findings. Unlike traditional writing tools, it shows you an editing view with red strikethrough corrections inline and numbered margin comments from each agent, adapting its critique style based on whether you're writing an article (strict), social media post (casual), or video script (pacing-focused). And now, with confidence I can publish more!
