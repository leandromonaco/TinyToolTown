Worried that you aren't meeting your quota for heart reactions in your team meetings? Heart‑It is your tiny-but-mighty sidekick for Microsoft Teams: a cheerful little toolbar that lets you fire off reactions without ever breaking your flow. Tap a global hotkey and BAM... hearts, laughs, claps, and surprises fly into your meeting like magic. It hangs out quietly, automatically finds your Teams window, and is always ready to help you celebrate, applaud, or lovingly chaos‑bomb a call with zero window‑switching.
