Kaprekar Explorer turns two delightfully strange fixed-point math routines into a colorful little single-page experience.

It lets people enter a valid seed, animate the subtraction steps, switch between the 4-digit 6174 mode and the 3-digit 495 mode, and share the result with deep links and generated share cards. I built it because Kaprekar's constants are the kind of tiny math rabbit hole that feels perfect for a playful, polished web toy.
