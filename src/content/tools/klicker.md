Klicker is a lightweight macOS menu bar app that adds audible feedback to every mouse click, including both left and right clicks. Built for people who enjoy tactile-feeling sound feedback, want better focus, or need cleaner click audio in screen recordings and live demos. Quick enable/disable, separate sounds for left and right click, 14 built-in sounds, support for custom audio files, volume control, and launch at login.
