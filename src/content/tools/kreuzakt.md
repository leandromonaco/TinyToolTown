Kreuzakt is a project that takes the best parts of [Paperless](https://docs.paperless-ngx.com/), drastically improves the OCR using VLLMs, and throws out 99% of the complexity. Take every boring document in your life and make them all instantly easy to find, and (optionally) let AIs search them to answer questions for you via a built-in MCP server.
