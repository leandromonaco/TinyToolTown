MoKa News is a stylish terminal-based RSS news aggregator that crafts AI-generated morning editorials from your favorite feeds. It supports multiple AI providers, smart date filtering, and an interactive TUI for browsing and publishing editorials. With features like keyword-focused summaries, scheduled refreshes, and OPML feed management, MoKa News makes staying informed both enjoyable and efficient.
