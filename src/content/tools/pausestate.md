PauseState is a calming breathing exercise app with built-in meditation tracking. Choose from preset patterns like Box Breathing (4-4-4-4), Relaxing 4-7-8, or Deep Calm — or create your own custom rhythm. Beautiful animated visuals and nature backgrounds keep you focused. After each session, track your daily streak, set minute goals, and watch your progress grow. All data stays private in your browser — no accounts, no servers, no tracking.
