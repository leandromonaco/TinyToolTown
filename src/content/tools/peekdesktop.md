PeekDesktop brings macOS Sonoma's "click wallpaper to reveal desktop" behavior to Windows 10 and 11. Click empty wallpaper to minimize open windows, interact with desktop icons without accidental triggers, then restore everything with a click on an app, the taskbar, or the wallpaper again. It's a small, fun system tray utility with x64 and ARM64 builds.
