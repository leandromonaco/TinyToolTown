PicCaptionr is your AI-powered caption sidekick.
Drop in your photos, and it instantly understands what’s happening — then crafts Instagram-ready captions complete with emojis, hashtags, and personality.

Whether you're a traveler, content creator, or just someone who never knows what to write under a photo — PicCaptionr saves you from the dreaded blinking cursor moment.

Available as console or desktop app on Windows, Linux, and macOS.
You bring the photos. PicCaptionr brings the vibe. 📸✨
