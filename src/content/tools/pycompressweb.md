I often take pictures with my camera, and do some edits in lightroom for good quality etc. When you share to social media, the algorithm does the image compression for you, but if you run you own blog, you need to think about the size, and the tradeoff between size and quality. This small web app, can be spun up with Docker Compose in 5 seconds, and it will bulk compress in 2 easy delightful steps for you :) and you can download a zip file with the pictures, you just specify the output size you want, and it gives you the best quality within those limits.
