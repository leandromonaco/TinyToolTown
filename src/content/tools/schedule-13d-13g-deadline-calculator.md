Schedule 13D / 13G Deadline Calculator is a small local-first web tool for planning common beneficial ownership reporting deadline scenarios. Pick a 13D or 13G trigger pattern, enter the relevant trigger/month-end/quarter-end date, add optional SEC closure dates, then copy a planning memo or CSV row.

I built it because these deadline windows are easy to lose inside filing checklists, holiday calendars, and spreadsheet notes. The tool is deliberately just the worksheet: no signup, no cookies, no analytics, no beacons, no external scripts, no uploads, and no browser storage. It links to SEC source materials and does not provide legal advice, securities advice, or EDGAR filing services.
