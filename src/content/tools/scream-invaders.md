A browser-based Space Invaders clone where your keyboard is useless and your dignity is optional. Your webcam tracks your face for movement (tilt left, ship goes left). Your microphone volume controls your weapon: quiet noise = single shot, yell = triple burst, full scream = screen-clearing beam that shakes the whole display. Built with Three.js and TensorFlow.js BlazeFace in a single HTML file. Zero dependencies to install, no build step. By wave 5 you'll be standing up. By wave 7 someone will come check on you.
