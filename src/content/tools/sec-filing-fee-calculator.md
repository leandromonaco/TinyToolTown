SEC Filing Fee Calculator is a tiny local-first web tool for estimating SEC filing fees with the FY2026 $138.10 per $1,000,000 rate. Enter an aggregate amount directly or calculate it from shares times price, subtract any prior fee offset, then copy a filing-fee memo or download an EX-FILING-FEES review row.

I built it because the fee math is simple but easy to bury in filing checklists, payment pages, and old spreadsheets. The delightful part is that the tool is just the worksheet: no signup, no cookies, no analytics, no beacons, no external scripts, no uploads, and no browser storage.
