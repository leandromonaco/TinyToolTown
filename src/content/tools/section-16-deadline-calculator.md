Section 16 Deadline Calculator is a tiny local-first web tool for estimating common SEC insider reporting target dates. Choose Form 3, Form 4, or Form 5, enter the trigger date, add any extra SEC closure dates, and copy a source-linked planning memo or CSV row for a legal, compliance, issuer-ops, finance, or EDGAR filing checklist.

I built it because Section 16 date checks are short and easy to miss: Form 3 uses a 10-day initial ownership window, Form 4 uses a two-business-day transaction reporting window, and Form 5 uses a 45-day fiscal-year-end catch-up window. The tool keeps that worksheet focused and local: no signup, no cookies, no analytics, no beacons, no external scripts, no uploads, and no browser storage.
