Shadr is a tiny Windows tray app that lets you push your screen brightness past your monitor’s limits—down for late-night work or up when daylight is fighting you. Pick from six levels (25%–150%), reset to 100% with a double-click, and stay up to date with built-in GitHub auto-updates—all in a lightweight, single EXE.
