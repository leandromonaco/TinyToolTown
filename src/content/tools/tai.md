You know the command. It does exactly what you need. You just can't remember it. `tai` does. It is for the commands you know exist but can't quite recall. Describe what you want in natural language, get the exact shell command back. Perfect for someone like me who tends to forget e.g. how to list local open ports saving me a time consuming roundtip to a chat agent on a different window.

Built on top of Github Copilot SDK.
