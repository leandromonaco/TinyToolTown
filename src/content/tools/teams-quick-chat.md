Teams Quick Chat is a lightweight Windows tray app that lets you instantly open a Microsoft Teams 1:1 chat with any pinned contact. With a sleek flyout UI, drag-and-drop reordering, and OneDrive sync, it keeps your contacts accessible and up to date across devices. No browser roundtrips—just fast, direct Teams chats from your tray.
