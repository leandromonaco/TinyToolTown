A simple desktop app for time-blocking your day. Drag to create blocks, move and resize them, colour-code by category, and everything saves automatically as readable markdown files.

It also has a toggleable/draggable widget window for the bottom right of your screen that shows the current task and how much time is left.

I wanted something similar to the Notion Calendar UI (formerly Cron), that I could use on client laptops. Most companies use Outlook, which isn't great for heavy time-blocking.
