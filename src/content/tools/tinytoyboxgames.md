Tiny Toybox Games is a magical 3D playroom that lives entirely in the browser, kids ages 3–6 tap a cozy bedroom scene, open a toybox, and tumble into a miniature forest world filled with popping bubbles and friendly sharks. I built it to prove that a rich, warm, child-safe experience can be created with zero assets, every mesh, material, particle effect, and sound is generated from pure TypeScript at runtime, with nothing stored on the device, ever. What makes it delightful is the first-tap promise: no matter where a child touches the screen, something beautiful happens, sparkles burst, owls hop, bubbles pop, combos chain, so there are no dead ends, no fail states, and no reading required, just pure sensory joy from the very first tap.
