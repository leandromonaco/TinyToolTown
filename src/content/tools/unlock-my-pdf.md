You have a PDF you own - a bank statement, a scanned form, a manual - and you cannot copy text from it, print it, or merge it with another PDF because it's locked. Unlock My PDF is a 30-second install, free, fully-local tool that drops an Unlock PDF button into your right-click menu. 

Pick a PDF --> RIght click --> Get an unlocked copy next to the original. Done.

Features
1. Right-click in Finder - no terminal needed after install
2. Batch support - select multiple PDFs, unlock all in one click
3. Local & private - your files never leave your Mac
4. Non-destructive - original is preserved; output is -unlocked.pdf
5. Tiny - one shell script, one Automator Quick Action, no daemons

Online "PDF unlocker" sites want you to upload private documents to a stranger's server. Desktop apps want $30 and a subscription.
