Value-Buddy helps consultants and freelancers stop guessing their project price. You describe what your work is worth to the client — not how long it takes — and it spits out three tiers of prices using one of two battle-tested curves: "Might As Well" (play it safe) or "Goldilocks" (go bold). Built on Jonathan Stark's value-pricing philosophy.

Why did you build it?
I kept doing the mental math on a napkin every time I had to quote a project and wanted to improve the original idea of Jonathan Stark.

Why is it delightful?
Because it has an AI tab powered by OpenRouter's free route that reads your project brief and tells you both what the project is worth to your client and which pricing curve to use. No backend, no subscription, no credits — just a static site whispering "you could charge more."
