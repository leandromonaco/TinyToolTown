VoiceSnippets is a cross-platform desktop app that lets you control your computer with your voice. Say a trigger word, and it instantly expands text snippets, fires keyboard shortcuts, or runs multi-step workflows. All powered locally by Whisper, with no cloud services involved. Perfect for developers who want to stay in flow without reaching for the keyboard.
