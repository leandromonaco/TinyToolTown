A CLI tool that pulls all your liked and bookmarked posts from Xiaohongshu (China's Instagram-meets-Pinterest), auto-tags them by category using AI, and saves everything as structured JSON. Built because I had 500+ liked posts and no way to find anything. Now I can search, filter by topic, and actually use the stuff I saved.
